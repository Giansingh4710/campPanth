 Welcome to Camp Panth, where visionaries turn into leaders and students into teachers through education and community building.

What is Camp Panth?

Camp Panth is an educational institutional platform

Camp Panth aims to provide a platform to experience the Gurus “Kashi”, focusing on educating attendees on various aspects of Gurmat from learning how to read Gurbani, understanding Sikh history and philosophy, and other related topics. These activities are taught through the traditional scholarly institution of Education called Sampardi Vidya.

The goal of the camp is to empower the participants to take ownership of their own spiritual development and to be an active member of the community.

Guru’s Kashi?

Kashi refers to a city in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, regarded as the spiritual capital of India. One typical comes to this city to embark on their education of traditional Indian scriptures and texts.

The Guru’s Kashi refers to a prophecy attributed to Guru Gobind Singh Ji where Guru Ji refers to a time when his Sikhs in great numbers will receive education. Education not only about their own faith, but faiths across the world so that they can be prepared to take the world into their care. This education will be given at their “Kashi”. This Kashi is independent of space and time, wherever a Sikh is given education of Gurmat there is the Gurus Kashi.
